Dragoon Skills Overview

Global Cooldown Actions (Single Target)

Icon Name Acronym Description
True Thrust TT Basic combo initiator for both the Chaos Thrust and the Full Thrust combo with 230 potency.
Raiden Thrust RT Direct upgrade to True Thrust after successfully landing the positional of the last combo action, i.e. Fang and Claw in the Chaos Thrust combo and Wheeling Thrust in the Full Thrust combo. The True Thrust button will temporarily be replaced by this 260 potency action. Grants a stack of Firstminds' Focus at Level 90.
Disembowel DE 250 potency action that combos off of True Thrust/Raiden Thrust and applies a 10% damage buff for 30s.
Chaos Thrust CT 260 potency action that combos off of Disembowel and applies a 40 potency Damage over Time effect to the target for 24s. Has to be executed from the targets rear for maximum damage. The DoT effect is not influenced by the positional.
Chaotic Spring CS An upgraded version of Chaos Thrust that automatically replaces Chaos Thrust on your hotbar at Level 86. Increases the potency to 280 and the DoT potency to 45.
Vorpal Thrust VT 280 potency action that combos off of True Thrust/Raiden Thrust
Full Thrust FT 400 potency action that combos off of Vorpal Thrust.
Heavens' Thrust HT An upgraded version of Full Thrust that automatically replaces Full Thrust on your hotbar at Level 86. Increases potency to 430.
Fang and Claw FC Blood of the Dragon must be active to use this combo action. Either used as the 4th combo action in the Full Thrust combo with 300 potency or as the 5th combo action in the Chaos Thrust combo with 400 potency (see Lance Mastery). Must be executed from the targets flank for maximum damage. Extends the duration of Blood of the Dragon by 10s.
Wheeling Thrust WT Blood of the Dragon must be active to use this combo action. Either used as the 4th combo action in the Chaos Thrust combo with 300 potency or as the 5th combo action in the Full Thrust combo with 400 potency (see Lance Mastery). Must be executed from the targets rear for maximum damage. Extends the duration of Blood of the Dragon by 10s.
Piercing Talon PT Uncomboed ranged action with 150 potency. Will not terminate an active combo if used between combo skills.

Global Cooldown Actions (Area of Effect)

Icon Name Acronym Description
Doom Spike DoS Basic combo initiator for the AoE combo with 110 potency. Deals damage to all enemies in a rectangular shape.
Draconian Fury DrF Direct upgrade to Doom Spike after using a comboed Coerthan Torment. The Doom Spike button will temporarily be replaced by this 130 potency action. Grants a stack of Firstmind’s Focus at Level 90.
Sonic Thrust SoT 120 potency action that combos off of Doom Spike. Deals damage to all enemies in a rectangular shape and grants the Power Surge buff for 30s, increasing damage dealt by 10%.
Coerthan Torment CoT 150 potency action that combos off of Sonic Thrust. Deals damage to all enemies in a rectangular shape and allows the execution of Draconian Fury.

off-Global Cooldown Actions

Icon Name Acronym Description
Jump - 320 potency action with a 30s cooldown. Jumps to the target and returns to the original position immediately - long animation and movement lock. Allows the execution of Mirage Dive within 15s after using Jump.
High Jump HJ Upgraded version of Jump with 400 potency and a reduced movement lock.
Spineshatter Dive SSD 250 potency action with a 60s cooldown. Jumps to the target - long animation lock.
Dragonfire Dive DFD Jumps to the target and deals 300 potency to all enemies in a 5y radius around it. 120s cooldown - long animation lock.
Mirage Dive MD 200 potency action that will add an eye to your Dragon Gauge when used while Blood of the Dragon is active. Only available after using (High) Jump.
Geirskogul GSK 260 potency action with a 30s cooldown that deals damage to all enemies in a rectangular shape. 30% reduced damage for all enemies hit after the first one. Enters Life of the Dragon when used with two Dragon Eyes open.
Nastrond NAS Replaces Geirskogul when in Life of the Dragon. 360 potency action that deals damage to all enemies in a rectangular shape and has a 10s cooldown. 30% reduced damage for all enemies hit after the first one.
Stardiver STD or SD Jumps to the target and deals damage to all enemies in 5y radius around it (500 potency for the first enemy, 30% less for all remaining enemies). Only available once per Life of the Dragon (30s cooldown) - very long animation lock.
Wyrmwind Thrust WWT 420 potency action with a 30s cooldown that deals damage to all enemies in a rectangular shape. 50% reduced damage for all enemies hit after the first one. Can only be executed when you have two Stacks of Firstminds' Focus
Elusive Jump EJ Jumps 15y backwards. Used to travel long distances quickly. 30s cooldown.

Buffs

Icon Name Acronym Description
Lance Charge LC Increases damage dealt by 10% for 20s. 60s cooldown
Dragon Sight DS Increases own damage dealt by 10% and the damage dealt by a specified target within 12y by 5% for 20s. 120s cooldown. Refer to the Basic Guide for tips on how to use this.
Battle Litany BL Increases the Critical Hit Rate by self and party members within 15y by 10% for 15s. 120s cooldown.
Life Surge LS Guarantees a Critical Hit on the next Global Cooldown Action within 5s. 45s cooldown. Also works on AoE actions. Absorbs 10% of the damage dealt from the buffed action as own HP
Current Tincture of Strength Pot Increases Strength by 10% for 30s. 270s cooldown.

Role Actions

Icon Name Acronym Description
Second Wind - Instant self-heal for 500 potency. 120s cooldown.
Leg Sweep - Stuns the target for 3s. 40s cooldown.
Bloodbath BB Heals self for 1/6 of all physical damage dealt for 20s. 90s cooldown.
Feint - Reduces target’s physical damage dealt by 10% and magixc damage by 5% for 10s. 90s cooldown.
Arm’s Length AL Grants knockback immunity for 6s (note that some knockbacks cannot be mitigated with this) and inflicts 20% slow for 15s on attackers when struck during this time. 120s cooldown.
True North TN Nullifies all positional requirements for 10s. 45s cooldown, can hold two charges.

Traits

Icon Name Acronym Description
Blood of the Dragon - Increases Jump potency to 320 and Spineshatter Dive potency to 250.
Lance Mastery - Enables the use of Fang and Claw or Wheeling Thrust as a 5th combo action and increases the potency of the 5th combo hit by 110 (380 -> 490).
Life of the Dragon LotD Enables the collection of Dragon Eyes by using Mirage Dive. Using Geirskogul at two Eyes will activate Life of the Dragon for 20s.
Jump Mastery - Upgrades Jump to High Jump.
Lance Mastery II - Increases the potency of True Thrust to 230, Vorpal Thrust to 280 and Disembowel to 250. Also enables the use of Raiden Thrust.
Blood of the Dragon Mastery - Extends Life of the Dragon duration to 30s.
Enhanced Coerthan Torment - Enables the use of Draconian Fury.
Enhanced Spineshatter Dive - Grants a second charge for Spineshatter Dive.
Lance Mastery III - Upgrades Chaos Thrust to Chaotic Spring and Full Thrust to Heavens' Thrust.
Enhanced Life Surge - Grants a second charge of Life Surge.
Lance Mastery IV - Increases Geirskogul potency to 250 and Nastrond potency to 350. Also enables the collection of Firstminds' Focus stacks.
